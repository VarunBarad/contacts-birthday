Privacy Policy

At Contacts Calendar, accessible from https://contacts-calendar.varunbarad.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Contacts Calendar and how we use it.

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.

Log Files

Contacts Calendar follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services' analytics. The information collected by log files include internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends and administering the site.

Google Account

While we do authenticate with your Google Account, we don't store any of your data anywhere with us. When you authenticate, we read the required data from your Google account and provide you with the calendar without storing anything on our machines.

Cookies

We only use cookies necessary to support Google authentication and maintaining your current session. No cookies are used to track/profile you or your activity.

Third Party Privacy Policies

Contacts Calendar doesn't use any third party service or advertiser so no third-party privacy policies apply.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This Privacy Policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website with regards to the information that they shared and/or collect in Contacts Calendar. This policy is not applicable to any information collected offline or via channels other than this website.
